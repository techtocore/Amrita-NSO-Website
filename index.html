About Amrita

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is an ”A Grade” Institution accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), an autonomous body established in 1994 by the University Grants Commission (UGC) of India to assess and accredit institutions of higher education in the country. Being one of the youngest institutions in India Amrita’s motto is to deliver up to date knowledge to the students, by providing state-of-the-art facilities and a peaceful atmosphere to impart knowledge.

About NSO

Given Amrita’s mission to awaken the technical drive of school students, Amrita’s National Science Olympiad encourages students to express their technical knowledge by providing a stage to showcase their ideas. Amrita’s drive for a sustainable future encourages students to focus on topics relevant to the current trends in the respective domains of Science and Technology.
