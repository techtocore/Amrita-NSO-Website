What is it all about?


Imagine sitting at your computer and controlling a video game character through a mall or market. isn't it fun? Imagine How AI & IOT will transform industries. What is your contribution to make life easier, faster and smarter without giving up values of sustainability, eco-friendliness and culture?   • There Shall be teacher from your school as contact point for a team

  • High School Students (class 5 to 8)
  • Higher Secondary School Students (class 9 to 12)

  • Minimum no of Student Participants: 2
  • Maximum no of Student Participants: 5
